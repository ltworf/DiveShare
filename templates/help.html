Purpose

This website lets you upload and share your divelogs, and add photos to them. You can use it to show your logs on your phone and share them with your friends online.

Usage

Open subsurface, File->Export and select DiveShare.

Privacy

Do not ever upload something that you wouldn't want to see spread all over the internet. This is a good general rule.
Dives marked as private will not be listed, but whoever possesses (or guesses) their id, will be able to see them.
